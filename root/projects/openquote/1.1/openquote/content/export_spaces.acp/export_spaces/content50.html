OpenQuote is the Open Source solution for providing insurance quotations over the web. Using a web interface, insurance companies can define any number of products or schemes to support any line of business. These can then be made instantly available for quotation to any proposer or other insurance intermediary who has web access.

OpenQuote is Open Source; distributed under the GNU General Public License. This means you can download it and use it freely without charge - as long as you comply with the license. Applied Industrial Logic - the original developers of OpenQuote -  offer comprehensive commercial support and consultancy services should you require them, but you are not obliged to use them.

 
Get a quote 

OpenQuote comes with a number of sample products to give a feel of its capabilities. You can get a quote from these right now by clicking the links.

    • Motor Plus - A reasonably complex personal motor product.
    • Equine Plus - Very similar in complexity to Motor Plus, but a different line.
    • Irish Broker PI - A Professional Indemnity product developed in conjunction with a London City broker. This product is designed for use by agents, so you need to be logged in as an agent in order to use it. Logging in as agent (password also 'agent') will give you access.
